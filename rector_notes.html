Rector's Note

Mr. Edward John Urio

It is my pleasure to welcome you to the Taffa Institute of Shipping and Logistics. This is a premier higher learning institution established in 2025 to provide training, research and consultancy services in shipping, logistics, technology, finance, business, and other related disciplines. The Institute has a favorable learning environment for students who join to pursue our undergraduate programs.

As the Institute is guided by well-defined philosophy and core values, our philosophical vision is to become an agent of change in public and private sector by offering quality market-driven academic and professional training, research and consultancy services. Attaining such philosophical quest, the entire community observes accountability, transparency and fairness, teamwork, integrity and innovativeness, trust, unity, diversity, gender equity, excellence and efficiency as our core values.

Furthermore, our motivated and competent staff work harmoniously in a community where life-long friendships and collegiality are encultured. As you browse through various pages of our site, you will see for yourself what we offer in the process of producing competent, industrial and innovative graduates. We hope you will consider the Taffa Institute of Shipping and Logistics as your next companion to your career success!

Our commitment to excellence in education is reflected in our state-of-the-art facilities, comprehensive curriculum, and expert faculty members who bring real-world experience to the classroom. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that fosters critical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and practical skills development.

At Taffa Institute, we understand the dynamic nature of the global shipping and logistics industry, and we continuously update our programs to meet the evolving demands of the sector. Our strong industry connections ensure that our graduates are well-prepared for successful careers and can make immediate contributions to their employers.

I encourage prospective students, parents, researchers, and industry partners to explore what Taffa Institute has to offer. Whether you are interested in pursuing an academic program, collaborating on research, or seeking professional development opportunities, we are here to support your aspirations and help you achieve your goals.

We look forward to welcoming you to our community and being part of your educational journey.
