OUR FACULTIES

Taffa Institute offers a range of specialized faculties dedicated to excellence in education, research, and innovation in their respective fields. Explore our faculties to learn more about our academic programs, research initiatives, and faculty members.

FACULTY OF COMPUTING AND TECHNOLOGY

The Faculty of Computing and Technology is dedicated to providing students with cutting-edge education in computing, information technology, and digital solutions for the shipping and logistics industry. Our programs emphasize both theoretical knowledge and practical skills to prepare graduates for successful careers.

Featured Programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Information Technology
  • Bachelor of Science in Supply Chain Information Systems
  • Master of Science in Logistics Information Systems
Learn More

FACULTY OF FINANCE AND BANKING

The Faculty of Finance and Banking is committed to developing financial professionals with specialized knowledge in shipping, logistics and supply chain finance. Our programs combine robust financial theory with practical applications specific to the maritime and logistics industries.

Featured Programs:

  • Bachelor of Finance in Shipping and Logistics
  • Bachelor of Banking and International Trade Finance
  • Master of Maritime Finance
Learn More

FACULTY OF MARITIME STUDIES

The Faculty of Maritime Studies provides comprehensive education in maritime operations, shipping management, and nautical sciences. With a focus on practical training and industry standards, we prepare students for careers in the global maritime sector, equipping them with the knowledge and skills needed for success.

Featured Programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Maritime Transport
  • Bachelor of Science in Shipping Management
  • Diploma in Nautical Studies
Coming Soon

FACULTY OF LOGISTICS AND SUPPLY CHAIN

The Faculty of Logistics and Supply Chain Management is dedicated to developing professionals who can design, implement, and manage efficient supply chain systems. Our curriculum integrates theoretical foundations with real-world applications, preparing students to address complex logistics challenges in a global context.

Featured Programs:

  • Bachelor of Science in Supply Chain Management
  • Bachelor of Science in Logistics Management
  • Master of Logistics and Supply Chain Management
