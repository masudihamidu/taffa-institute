Historical Background

Since its foundation in 2025, the Taffa Institute of Shipping and Logistics stands as a leading higher learning institution in the region specializing in shipping, logistics, and supply chain management education. The Institute has been dedicated to excellence in teaching, research, and consultancy services. Currently, the Institute enrolls about 8,000 students in undergraduate programmes.

At Taffa Institute, students are inspired and challenged to investigate critical issues of the 21st century in areas of shipping and logistics management, supply chain operations, maritime safety, international trade, customs operations, and information technology. Taffa offers an unparalleled student experience, both in and out of the classroom.

The Institute has three faculties that award certificate, diploma, and degree qualifications. The Institute also offers short courses on a regular basis to address specific industry needs in the market. Up to date, the Institute has trained over 15,000 alumni who hold key positions in both private and public sectors across East Africa and beyond.

Since its inception, Taffa Institute has grown from a small specialized training center to a comprehensive higher learning institution with modern facilities, qualified teaching staff, and industry-relevant programs. Our continuous adaptation to technological advancements and industry demands has positioned us as the preferred institution for shipping and logistics education in the region.

Mission

To provide quality academic and professional training that nurtures creativity and innovation through integrated training, research and consultancy in shipping, logistics and related disciplines.

Vision

To be a reputable world-class higher learning institution that is responsive to global development needs through provision of knowledge and competences in shipping, logistics management and related disciplines.

Philosophy

The Institute will continue to be an agent for change in public and private sector development by offering quality market-driven academic and professional training, as well as research and consultancy services in the fields of shipping, logistics, and supply chain management.

Core Values

In line with its Vision and the need to fulfill its Mission and live to its philosophy, the Taffa Institute adheres to eight core values, abbreviated as "ATTITUDE" as described below:

ACCOUNTABILITY

Demonstrating high degree of accountability to our stakeholders and to the community for the mandated functions of the Institute.

TRANSPARENCY AND FAIRNESS

Being transparent in our work and dealings, standing ready for public scrutiny as well as applying uniform rules and standards.

TEAMWORK

Promoting the culture of team spirit and compassion in order to create synergy in operations.

INTEGRITY AND INNOVATIVENESS

Demonstrating high fidelity, honesty, faithful and trustworthy behavior in all our operations as well as embracing new technologies.

TRUST

Seeking to build trust in every interaction with customers and other stakeholders.

UNITY

Promoting unity for effective teamwork and collaborative solutions.

DIVERSITY & GENDER EQUITY

Embracing diversity and gender equity in the life of the Institute to promote balance and utilize human resource potentials.

EXCELLENCE AND EFFICIENCY

Delivering prompt and exemplary services to Institute's stakeholders at all times.
